Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 6, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At VimNav, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: When you join our waitlist, submit feedback, or contact us, we collect your name and email address.
  • Usage Preferences: Information about your browser preferences, Vim experience level, and how you plan to use VimNav.
  • Feedback and Communications: Any messages, feedback, or support requests you send us.

Information We Don't Collect

VimNav does not collect:

  • Personal browsing history or website content
  • Sensitive personal information (passwords, financial data, etc.)
  • Information from third-party websites you visit
  • Device identifiers or tracking cookies for advertising purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Product Communication: To notify you about VimNav's launch, updates, and special offers
  • Customer Support: To respond to your questions, feedback, and support requests
  • Product Development: To understand user needs and improve VimNav's features
  • Marketing Analytics: To measure the effectiveness of our email campaigns and understand user engagement

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Limited Third-Party Services

We may share your information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business:

  • Email Campaign Providers: We use email marketing services to send you notifications and updates. These providers help us analyze campaign performance, track email opens, clicks, and engagement metrics to improve our communications.
  • Form Processing: We use Netlify Forms to process waitlist signups and feedback submissions.
  • Website Hosting: Our website is hosted by third-party services that may have access to basic website analytics.

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the specific services they provide to us.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we have about you
  • Correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information
  • Unsubscribe: You can opt out of marketing communications at any time using the unsubscribe link in our emails

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@vimnav.dev.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Waitlist information is retained until VimNav launches and for a reasonable period afterward to honor early bird pricing commitments.

International Users

VimNav is operated from the United States. If you are accessing our services from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located and our central database is operated.

Children's Privacy

VimNav is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new privacy policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@vimnav.dev
  • General Support: support@vimnav.dev
  • Feedback Form: Submit feedback
